Who We Are
Est. 2011 · Houston, TX

REAL IRON.
REAL RESULTS.

Iron Underground was born in a warehouse on the south side of Houston by lifters who were tired of commercial gyms built for selfies. We set the weights down and built something different — a place where barbells bend and egos don't.

Over a decade later, we've produced powerlifting champions, IFBB pros, and thousands of everyday athletes who just wanted to get seriously strong.

  • 30,000 sq ft of hardcore training floor space
  • Professional powerlifting platforms with Eleiko and Texas bars
  • Dedicated strongman yard with competition implements
  • On-site nutrition bar and recovery lounge
  • 24/7 member access with keycard entry
